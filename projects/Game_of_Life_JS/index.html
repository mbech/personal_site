Game of Life

Instructions:

  1. Click cells in the grid to make them alive (green) or dead (black).
  2. Click 'Advance State' to update the board.
  3. 'Auto-advance' will automatically advance the state at the set time interval.
  4. 'Clear Board' kills all cells
  5. 'Randomize' flips a coin to determine each cell's state.

See the Wikipedia page for more info: wikipedia.org/wiki/Conway's_Game_of_Life
